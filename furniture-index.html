Our Story

Where Craft Meets Elegance

Maison Arca was founded with a singular vision: to bring the warmth and soul of handcrafted furniture into the modern home. We work with master artisans and sustainably sourced materials to create heirloom-quality pieces.

From our atelier in Ashford, every piece passes through hands that have spent years perfecting their craft — combining traditional joinery techniques with contemporary design sensibility.
